About

Welcome to SQUARE EYES, where we connect film-lovers with independent filmmakers. Our mission is to support small, talented filmmakers by giving them a platform to showcase their work and reach a wider audience.

At SQUARE EYES, we believe that everyone should have access to great films, regardless of where they come from or how big their budget is. We're passionate about discovering and sharing unique, high-quality films that you won't find anywhere else.

Our team is made up of film enthusiasts who are dedicated to curating the best independent films from around the world. We believe that film has the power to inspire, entertain, and educate, and we're committed to promoting diverse voices and perspectives in cinema.

Whether you're a fan of drama, comedy, horror, or documentary, you'll find something to love on SQUARE EYES. We offer a wide range of films from up-and-coming filmmakers, so you can discover new talent and support independent artists.

Thank you for choosing SQUARE EYES as your go-to source for independent films. We're excited to share our passion for cinema with you, and we look forward to growing our community of film-lovers and filmmakers alike.
